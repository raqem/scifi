The Beard, The Babe, and The Bear

April- June 2015

J Darris Mitchell, his darling wife, and a stuffed bear intrepidly travelled through Europe on a shoe-string budget. Goats were milked, trains were missed, and former brothels were slept in. Rest assured shenanigans transpired in at least six countries.

The Bearded Kaiju

June 2014- April 2015

The Bearded Kaiju chronicles a year of life in Hida-Takayama. J. Darris Mitchell teaches English, participates in bizarre festivals, and undergoes surgery in a wild mountain town. If you have ever wanted to teach abroad or explore Japan's hidden countryside this is the blog for you.
